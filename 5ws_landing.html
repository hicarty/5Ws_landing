Our Story

Where intelligence and fashion meet.

Founded by Rumbie Makonise after recognising the gap between fashion management theory and real-world practice, 5 Ws of Fashion is a research and events talent agency that gives conscious consumers, brands, and the next generation of fashion professionals a space to meet, learn, and lead.

We don't just host events. We create environments where unpopular opinions can be spoken freely, where businesses align authentically with sustainable values, and where Leicester reclaims its rightful place at the forefront of British fashion.

What We Do

Research-led.
Event-activated.
Commercially purposeful.

01
Fashion Research
Underpinned by academic rigour and market insight, our research informs event topics, brand partnerships, and consumer messaging strategies.
02
Event Curation
Immersive, education-led experiences that keep the client central while creating genuine discovery moments for every guest in the room.
03
Brand Partnerships
We partner exclusively with brands that authentically align with sustainable values — and we'll negotiate hard to make sure the deal works for everyone.
04
Talent Pipeline
Our volunteer and mentorship structure creates a genuine pathway from fashion student to international fashion week experience.
05
Conscious Consumer Activation
We translate sustainability from buzzword to lived behaviour through events that feel exclusive, educational, and genuinely aspirational.
06
Academic Liaison
With De Montfort University partnerships and speakers including SDG Impact researchers, we ground every conversation in evidence.
